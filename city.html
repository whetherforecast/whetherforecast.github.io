About

                

Welcome to Whether Forecast! We're here to give you advice on whether you should wear flip flops or rain boots, eat a picnic sandwiches or a hearty stew, relax at home or venture outside. Whether Forecast uses real-time weather data to help you find the best things to do, wear, and eat.                 

                

Whether Forecast was created by Geirthana Sivagnanalingam, Christina Liu, Jule Schatz, and Mary Douglass Baum during MHacks at the University of Michigan in February 2016.
